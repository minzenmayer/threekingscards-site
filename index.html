Who we are

Meet the Three Kings (and their queen)

A dad, two little boys, and the mom who keeps them all upright (mostly). We started Three Kings Cards at our kitchen table because we love trading cards and wanted the boys to learn how to build something good, then give it away.

Every pack they help fill and every Scribblin they draw is part of that. Buy one, and you are in on it too.

Where the packs go

For a kid having a hard day

Hospitals, shelters, hard stretches. A pack of cards can turn one rough afternoon into a good one.

You won't know the kid who opened your pack, but you'll follow the whole journey here.
